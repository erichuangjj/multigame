Monster Supervisor

Command your monster army, collect power-ups, and survive increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

Gameplay Overview

Monster Command

In Monster Supervisor, you'll take control of a diverse army of monsters, each with unique abilities and powers. Direct your monsters strategically to overcome increasingly difficult enemy waves while collecting resources to strengthen your forces.

Survival Mechanics

As the game progresses, enemy waves become more challenging. Collect power-ups, upgrade your monsters, and develop strategic formations to survive. How long can you last against the onslaught?

Game Features

Diverse Monster Types

Choose from multiple monster classes, each with unique attacks, abilities, and upgrade paths to suit different play styles.

Dynamic Environments

Battle across various landscapes that affect gameplay, offering different challenges and strategic opportunities.

Progression System

Unlock new monsters, abilities, and power-ups as you play, creating a rewarding progression system that keeps you coming back for more.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD - Move your character
  • Space - Special ability
  • 1-4 - Select different monsters
  • E - Interact with objects

Mobile Controls

  • Left Side - Virtual joystick for movement
  • Right Side - Action buttons
  • Swipe - Change selected monster
  • Tap - Interact with objects

Survival Tips

  • •

    Focus on upgrading your strongest monsters early in the game to establish a solid defense.

  • •

    Don't neglect resource collection - the power-ups can make the difference between victory and defeat.

  • •

    Learn the attack patterns of different enemy types to develop effective counter-strategies.

  • •

    Balance your monster team with different abilities to handle various situations.
