Controls

Keyboard

  • WASD - Move
  • Space - Special
  • 1-4 - Select monster
  • E - Interact
  • Q - Quick swap
  • R - Reload/Reset

Mobile

  • Left side - Move
  • Right side - Actions

  • Hold - Charge attack
